Questions & Answers

Here are some of the most common questions regarding our Business English program.

How do I schedule classes? +

Contact theenglishprofessional@gmail.com to request a free 15-minute Skype meeting in which we can discuss your English development goals and scheduling.

How do I pay for my classes? +

Payments are made through PayPal. Simply go to The English Professional Rates page and make payments in US dollars before your classes begin.

Which program will I use for my online classes? +

Classes are conducted via Skype. Skype is easy and free to download and runs on the most common platforms (Microsoft Windows, macOS, Linux, Android, iOS, etc.). After scheduling your classes, find theEnglishProfessional on Skype and send a request to be added as a contact.
