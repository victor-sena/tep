Online Classes

Improve Conversational Skills

Having a conversation in a second or third language can be very challenging. Native speakers use many slang/popular expressions that are hard to understand. We will have informal conversations and learn common expressions used in conversational, business English.

Increase Vocabulary

A greater knowledge of workplace vocabulary and expressions will help you feel more confident in speaking and writing. Whatever your career field, we will use material that will help you reach your goals.

Improve Writing

English is the Internet’s universal language and the most common language in business. We offer editing services as well as online writing coaching to help improve your letters, emails, and resume.

Increase TOEIC Score

Many employers require a high score on the Test of English for International Communication. We determine ways you can increase your score and more effectively prepare for the test, ensuring you have the skills you need.

On-the-Job Performance

Added practice to communicate more effectively on the job. Programs include accent reduction to improve presentation skills, vocabulary development, writing clarity, and American business cultural awareness.

Job Support

Keep Career on Point

Living in a global world with English as the common corporate language means continual practice is imperative. Flexible and personalized online classes via Skype or Hangouts, even using your smartphone while on the road.

Interview Practice

Answering common interview questions in English can be stressful. We will discuss interview techniques and run mock interview practices so you feel relaxed and confident. (60-minute class)

Hire-Me Package

Looking for a job can be stressful. We will edit your cover letter and resume and help you prepare for your interview. Includes a 60-minute mock interview class + complete editing support.
